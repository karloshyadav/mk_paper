Process

Step-by-Step Production Flow

A disciplined process that prioritizes fiber integrity, sheet uniformity, and clean finishing.
  • Fiber sourcing & prep: Responsible input selection and sorting for stable pulp consistency.
  • Pulping & refining: Controlled refining to achieve strength and formation.
  • Sheet formation: Uniform web formation for consistent GSM.
  • Drying & surface treatment: Stable moisture control and finish.
  • Cutting & packing: Precision conversion and protective packaging.
